About Dental Point

One doctor. One clinic. No black box dentistry.

Dental Point was founded and is personally run by Dr. Adnan Qadir Khan in Rawalpindi, built around one idea: patients make better decisions about their own teeth when they actually understand what's happening in their mouth not just what it costs.

Where it started

Dr. Adnan Qadir Khan opened Dental Point after years of hearing the same complaint from patients: they'd leave appointments unsure what was actually wrong with them, or why a treatment cost what it did. Dental Point was built to fix that starting with the tools you've already used on this site, like the symptom checker and the price estimator, designed around what patients actually wish they knew before walking in.

How it works

Every patient at Dental Point is treated personally by Dr. Adnan no handing off between different dentists partway through a treatment plan. Every diagnosis is explained in plain language before any work starts, with a clear price range agreed on up front, no surprises at the counter.
