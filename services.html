Every service, explained

01

Cleanings & check-ups

A routine visit includes scaling to remove plaque and tartar, polishing, and a full oral exam including a quick screening for early signs of decay, gum disease, or oral cancer. Recommended every six months.

30–45 minPKR 2,500–5,000
02

Root canal therapy

When decay or injury reaches the nerve inside a tooth, we remove the infected tissue, clean the canal, and seal it saving the natural tooth instead of extracting it. Done under local anesthesia; most patients feel immediate relief from the pain that brought them in.

1–2 visitsPKR 15,000–35,000
03

Veneers & whitening

Thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of your teeth to fix chips, gaps, or discoloration permanently. For a less invasive option, in clinic whitening lifts stains from coffee, tea, or smoking in a single session.

Veneers: PKR 18,000–45,000/toothWhitening: PKR 8,000–20,000
04

Invisalign

Clear, removable aligners that gradually straighten teeth without metal brackets or wires. We map your treatment with a 3D scan up front, so you see the projected result before committing.

6–18 monthsPKR 180,000–350,000
05

Implants

A titanium post placed in the jaw replaces a missing tooth's root, topped with a crown that looks and functions like a natural tooth. The most durable option for replacing a missing tooth long term.

3–6 months totalPKR 60,000–150,000
06

Emergency care

Sudden pain, a knocked out or broken tooth, or facial swelling shouldn't wait for a scheduled slot. We hold same day emergency openings every day we're open.
